Required Documents

  • IELTS / TOEFL
  • Transcript of +2 or Proficiency Certificate Level
  • Provisional certificate
  • SLC certificate
  • Character certificate
  • Recommendation Letter - 2 copies from the latest School
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Sponsorship Letter or Affidavit of Support
  • Relation Certificate
  • Bank Balance Certificate
  • In the case of Loan, Loan sanction letter provided in the name of student
  • Property Valuation
  • Tax Clearance Certificate of Property
  • Land/Building/Vehicle or any other assets' legal ownerships documents
  • Income from Salary (Job certificate with Tax Statement if possible)
  • Income from Rent (Agreement paper/ ward certify certificate with Tax Statement)
  • In the case of business:-
  •      Income from Business ( Reg and PAN certificate, Auditors Reports and Tax statement)
  •      CA Valuation Certificate
  • Income from Agriculture (Ward Certification) - Notarized copy only
  • From Government:-
  •        No objection letter
  •        Police Report (Optional)
  •        Ward Recommendation Letter (Optional)
  •        Extra Curricular Activities certificate (Optional)
  • For VISA:-
  •        Copy of Passport
  •        Visa application form
  •        4 Piece of passport size photo
  •        Visa application letter
  •        Statement of Purpose
  •        Medical Report
Note:
*Documents should be made in your respective municipality office of permanent address.
*Do not make any fake documents. This may directly affect your visa.
*All the above documents should only be submitted in notarized copy.
