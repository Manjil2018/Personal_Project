Required Documents

  • Standard Aptitute Test (SAT) / American College Testing (ACT) - For Scholarship.
  • SAT Subject Test - For Scholarship (required by some highly reputed colleges).
  • TOEFL/IELTS - To measure the english language proficency for attending a college.
  • Personal Essay - Essay containing your personal story (Required by CommonApp Colleges).
  • Supplement Essay - Question asked by college (Required by few CommonApp Colleges).
  • Statement Of Purpose (SOP) - Essay containing reason to continue further studies.
  • Attested Documents - Results attested through USEF - Nepal.
  • International Student Financial Aid Application (ISFAA)** - Document to qualify for need based financial aid.
  • Certificate of Fianance (COF)*** - To prove the ability of paying tuition fees.
  • Letter of Recommendation - From Counselor/Principal, Teachers.
  • Certificates - Supporting materials for your documents.
Note:
**International Students do not qualify for FAFSA or any federal related funds.
**ISFAA is not accepted by every colleges
**Colleges may require CSS instead of ISFAA
***Some college might ask for it before giving the financial aid decision.
