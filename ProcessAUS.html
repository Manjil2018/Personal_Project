- Appear for IELTS/TOEFL.
- Research about the universities in Australia.
- If you already have relative then ask for their location and choose the university which will be nearest to you.
- Look for the available courses and their requirements.
- Check the fees of the colleges; whether you can afford or not.
- Check the scholarship section, some universities/colleges might provide it according to your qualification.
- Choosing the university with high tuition fees might be out of the reach.
- Check the list of authorised agents on the Apply section and visit the one nearest to you. (Applying through agents might save your application fee)
- After choosing the right university, write a Statement of Purpose and submit the photocopies of your transcripts, character certificate, migration certificate to the consultancy.
- -Then wait for your offer letter which might usually take upto 2-3 weeks.
-After receiving your offer letter start preparing your financial documents.
-Some universities/colleges might require the bank balance of 3 months, whereas some might not.
-Higher the course level you will choose, you are increasing your chance of visa. But, higher the course level higher the tuition fees will be. - A
